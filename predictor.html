World Cup Predictor

A couple weeks ago, Kimono introduced our World Cup API to the world. In the past few weeks, we've seen some awesome hacks built using our API, like this one and this one. We've put together a simple World Cup predictor using our own API below. Enjoy!

Select two teams below and we'll predict the winner of the match. One of our founders is a quantum physicist but we still can't guarantee 100% accuracy. Maybe you can use Kimono to do better?
